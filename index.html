What We Offer

IELTS

International English Language Testing System

We provide finest IELTS training by expert trainers keeping up with the changes in the industry. Trainers at Brythonic Academy follow a pattern based speaking and writing modules made easy to follow for even non english medium background candidates. Weekly mock exams provide the students and the trainer with the step by step improvement graph. Online and classroom teaching is provided for candidates according to convenience.

OET

Occupational English Test


OET training is provided by experienced faculties who have nursing and medical background to allow our students with the best learning experience.

PTE

Pearson Test of English


Pearson Test of English is accepted at universities, colleges and governments worldwide. It is a compter-based english language test trusted by several international universities. We provide personalised computer training with frequentmock exams to analyse improvement of students. When meeting your dreams to study or work abroad choosing PTE is the wisest move.
